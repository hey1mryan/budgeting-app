About Us
Centsable is an app that is designed to help people budget and save their money. We'll take your expenses and subtract it from your total income, and then calculate a recommended maximum amount to spend and a recommended minimum amount to save! Why us, you may ask? We're different from other budget planner apps. We can help you search Amazon for any product with a price under our recommended maximum amount to spend.

We were founded on August 9, 2024 by a group of four young men aspiring to be software engineers.
